MAKAO HOTELS

We offer various types of room from suites,deluxe,standard and parlour.In this page we are going to show you their images and brief descriptions about them.

Suites

This is the most expensive room provided by the hotel. It has one or more bedrooms depending on the one that fits best for you.It also has a strong emphasis on grand in room decoration and high quality amenities.

Parlour

This establishments is not meant for sleeping purpose. It is where people can sit,talk and relax.
We have double-sided sofas to create distinct seating groups that orien the space toward conversation. This helps relax the room plus we have also added lighter,whimsical pieces making it less daunting for our guests.

Deluxe

These are modern decorated and can accomodate upto 2 persons.
They are totally soundproofed and equipped with high tech comforts such as high speed internet access, USB ports,smart TV, room cleaning touch system and private hydromassage bathtub.
It is larger than a standard room.

Standard room

This is the cheapest room we have at Makao hotels. It is a type of single room,which has two queen-size beds.
It includes all kinds of basic facilities such as table,chair,desk,cupboard,dressing table,DVD player, television,coffee maker and a private bathroom.
